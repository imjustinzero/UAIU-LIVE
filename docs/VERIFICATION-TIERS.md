# Verification Tiers (Credits)

## Tier 1 — Fully Verified
UVS certified + human field verification completed by a verified partner.

## Tier 2 — Platform Verified
UVS certified + IoT + satellite verification, but no human field verification.

## Tier 3 — Registry Only
Registry confirmed only; no IoT/satellite/human verification.

## Tier 4 — Unverified
Registry not confirmed or verification pending.

## Determination logic
Tier is calculated from:
- UVS certification status
- IoT data presence
- Satellite cross-validation status
- Human verification completion
- Registry confirmation

## Upgrading tiers
- Tier 4/3 → Tier 2: complete UVS + IoT + satellite checks.
- Tier 2 → Tier 1: complete human field verification with a verified partner.

## Why tier matters for price
Higher-tier listings have stronger diligence signals and typically support higher buyer confidence, faster conversions, and premium pricing.
