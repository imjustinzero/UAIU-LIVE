# UAIU Verified Claim Legal Shield

## What the Verified Claim Certificate covers
- The exact claim text submitted by the organization.
- Publication URL/date and supporting retirement references.
- Verification state at the time of certificate issuance.

## How to register a public carbon claim
1. Submit claim details to `POST /api/claims/register`.
2. Include supporting credit retirement IDs and total tonnes.
3. Receive a certificate number when verification is complete.

## Evidence package for legal defense
- Claim text and publication context.
- Supporting retirements and tonnes mapping.
- Audit chain pointer and (optional) legal opinion hash.
- Public verification endpoint for third-party validation.

## Jurisdiction considerations
- Claims laws vary by jurisdiction and regulator.
- Coordinate with local counsel for disclosure wording.
- UAIU evidence is technical verification support, not legal advice.

## What UAIU certifies and what it does not
- UAIU certifies traceability and supportability of submitted claim evidence.
- UAIU does not certify future outcomes, financial projections, or legal outcomes.
