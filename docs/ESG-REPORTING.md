# UAIU.LIVE/X ESG Reporting Guide

## TCFD Alignment
The ESG dashboard surfaces governance, strategy, risk management, and metrics evidence links sourced from audit, trust, and export endpoints.

## GHG Protocol Scope 1/2/3
Per-organization offset tracking is provided in `/api/esg/metrics` with Scope 1/2/3 offsets, net-zero progress indicators, and year-over-year comparisons.

## ISO 14064 Verification Process
ISO-aligned evidence is generated via compliance documents and audit-backed settlement/retirement exports.

## Audit Chain Support for ESG Disclosures
UAIU.LIVE/X audit chain provides cryptographic linkage from purchase to settlement to retirement certificate, including hash algorithm provenance and rotation history.
