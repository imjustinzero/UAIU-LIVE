# Field Verifier Guide (UAIU.LIVE/X)

## 1) Register as a Field Verifier
- Create your UAIU.LIVE/X verifier account.
- Complete profile and region coverage details.
- Open **Credential Wallet** (`/x/verifier/credentials`) and upload certifications.

## 2) Use the Field App Offline
- Open `/x/field-app` and load your active engagement while connected.
- Project checklist and context are cached on your device.
- Observations, photos, and notes are queued locally when offline.
- Sync resumes automatically or manually once signal returns.

## 3) GPS Boundary Verification
- The app compares your live GPS position to project boundaries.
- Entry/exit events are logged with timestamp + coordinates.
- Each boundary event creates an audit-chain record for traceability.

## 4) Photo Documentation Standards
For each required field photo:
- GPS coordinates embedded.
- Timestamp embedded.
- Compass direction + altitude captured.
- Project overlay metadata applied.
- Content hash generated before evidence-vault submission.

## 5) Checklist Completion
- Checklist is generated from project type, required standard, and anomaly history.
- Every item supports Pass / Fail / Needs Follow-Up.
- Photo-required items must include evidence before submission unlocks.

## 6) Submit Field Report
- After all checklist items are complete, tap **Submit Field Report**.
- Platform drafts report from observations, photos, GPS logs, and IoT period data.
- Add voice narrative sections and apply digital signature.
- Submission writes an audit-chain event and stores report artifacts.

## 7) Quality Score Methodology
Quality score combines:
- Accuracy Rate (30%)
- Field Coverage Rate (20%)
- Documentation Quality (20%)
- Response Time (15%)
- Outcome Tracking (10%)
- Credential Strength (5%)

Scores are recalculated daily and published as numeric score + grade.

## 8) Legacy Dashboard
Open `/x/verifier/legacy` to view:
- Lifetime tonnes verified
- Country/project footprint
- Institutional buyer impact
- Methodology attribution and citations

## 9) Share Your Impact
Use dashboard share tools to:
- Generate a social impact card
- Download as PNG
- Add to professional profile and buyer-facing materials
