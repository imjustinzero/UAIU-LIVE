# Verifier Reputation on UAIU.LIVE/X

## How reputation scores are calculated
Composite score (0-100):
- Accuracy rate: 35%
- Response time: 20%
- Field visit frequency: 20%
- Dispute rate (inverse): 15%
- Specialization match: 10%

## How to improve your reputation score
- Increase post-audit confirmed accuracy.
- Shorten verification cycle time.
- Improve field visit consistency per project-year.
- Lower dispute incidence through stronger evidence quality.
- Build specialization performance in target credit categories.

## What AAA means vs AA vs A
- AAA: top-tier consistency, low disputes, highest trust profile.
- AA: strong performance with minor variance.
- A: good baseline performance, improvement opportunities remain.

## How buyers use reputation scores
- Directory ranking drives verifier discoverability.
- Institutional buyers can prioritize premium verification lanes.
- High-grade verifier badges increase credit confidence and pricing power.
