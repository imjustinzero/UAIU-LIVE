# UVS Governance Framework

## Standards Committee Charter
The committee governs UVS methodology, voting, and market-integrity controls with published minutes and disclosures.

## Conflict of Interest Policy
Members disclose financial, advisory, and ownership ties; recusals are mandatory on implicated votes.

## Amendment Process
Proposed -> Open Comment -> Voting -> Approved/Rejected. Votes are immutably logged.

## Appeals Procedure
Suppliers or buyers may appeal suspensions/revocations with evidence package references and independent review.

## Suspension & Revocation
Triggered by threshold breaches, fraud flags, legal holds, or sanctions exposure; actions are publicly documented.
